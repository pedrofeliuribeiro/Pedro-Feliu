Course Description
======

The course aims to present the main themes of studies of Foreign Policy Analysis (FPA), characterized both by a consolidated bibliography in the area, as well as by recent texts and emerging agendas. Different ontological and epistemological approaches will be studied, with a strong emphasis on research design and methods covered in the specialized literature. From an empirical point of view, several cases are analyzed such as Brazil, Argentina, United States, South Africa, China, Liberia, Sweden, United Kingdom, Egypt, Nigeria, Venezuela, Rwanda, among others. Compared studies are also explored in the course.
